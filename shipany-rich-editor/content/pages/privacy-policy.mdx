## Introduction

Welcome to Rich Editor (editor.shipany.com), a modern writing workspace for creators and teams. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to operate and improve Rich Editor:

1. **Account Information (optional)**
   - **What We Collect**: Name, email address, or other details you provide if you create an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account and provide customer support.

2. **Content and Drafts**
   - **What We Collect**: Content you create within Rich Editor.
   - **Purpose**: To provide editing, version history, and export features. Drafts are stored locally in your browser by default.

3. **Usage Details**
   - **What We Collect**: Feature usage, interactions, and performance diagnostics.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze engagement and improve the product experience.

4. **Device Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Device type, operating system, browser type, and basic technical metadata.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize compatibility and reliability.

5. **Cookies**
   - **What We Collect**: Small files that help remember preferences and improve usability.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance functionality and personalize your experience.

## Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to safeguard your information. Drafts are stored locally in your browser by default. If additional storage options are enabled in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests.
- To protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the service.
- To provide services through trusted partners under confidentiality agreements.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
